Technical CV

Hey, I’m Utkarsh — a data-native generalist with a math backbone and a builder’s instinct.
This space is part portfolio, part journal, and part blueprint — a living document of how I think, ship, and solve.

🧠 What I Bring

I’m deeply curious. I chase elegant structure in complex systems. I enjoy the grind — data wrangling, weird edge cases, modeling trade-offs — but I believe most in the why of building.

I thrive in high-context, low hand-holding environments where the work is uncertain and the pace is fast.

Things I do well:

  • Model behavior — from user retention to failure states.
  • Find signal — even in messy, inconsistent, first-draft data.
  • Communicate complexity — clear dashboards, clean visuals, tight documentation.
  • Stay scrappy — I don't wait for perfect; I iterate until true.

📂 What You’ll Find Here

  • Projects in machine learning, statistical modeling, and data storytelling.
  • Explorations in mathematical thinking that inform how I debug and design.
  • A visual and technical style that favors clarity, consistency, and control.
  • My resume — distilled.
  • My blog — unfinished, but honest.

🤝 What I’m Looking For

I want to work with people building real things — products that matter, questions that bite back, spaces where data isn’t gimmick but core to decision-making.

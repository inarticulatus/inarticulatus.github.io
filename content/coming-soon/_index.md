### About the theme

After going through the [Hugo Showcase](https://gohugo.io/showcase/) for a theme, I was inspired to create a simple yet beautiful portfolio theme that can cater to the likes of analysts, researchers, journalists etc, or someone who needs a simple, crisp blogpage. This gave birth to __paper-pro__. 
This theme takes inspiration from [Edward Tufte's](https://www.edwardtufte.com/tufte/) book design trends for the general theme, however the color combination and font is inspired by Financial Times' `salmon-pink` and elegant serif fonts. This combination lends a certain professional, fact oriented feel to the website. 

In future updates, I'll try to create dashboard theme and blog pages with similar combination.  


It will be difficult to customize parts/sections of the website due to how it is structured, but I have an idea on how to go about resolving that, and would attempt to implement that next.
